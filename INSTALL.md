# Installation Guide

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Load the Extension
1. Open Google Chrome
2. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
3. Enable **"Developer mode"** (toggle in the top-right corner)
4. Click **"Load unpacked"**
5. Navigate to and select the `Quotes` folder
6. The extension will load and you'll see it in your toolbar!

### Step 2: First Use
1. Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
2. Click **"Refresh Now"** to fetch quotes
3. You'll see quotes for the default currencies:
   - EURUSD (Euro to Dollar)
   - EURRUB (Euro to Russian Ruble)
   - USDRUB (Dollar to Russian Ruble)
   - CADRUB (Canadian Dollar to Russian Ruble)
   - BTCUSD (Bitcoin to Dollar)

### Step 3: Watch the Badge
The extension icon will display the current **EURUSD** rate directly on the icon badge (in green).

### Step 4: Customize Currencies (Optional)
1. Click the extension icon popup
2. Click the **⚙️ Settings** button
3. Select/deselect currencies to customize your tracked pairs:
   - Default pairs: EURUSD, EURRUB, USDRUB, CADRUB, BTCUSD
   - Optional pairs: GBPUSD, JPYUSD, AUDUSD
4. Click **"Save Settings"**
5. Quotes will refresh with your new selection!

## How It Works

**Automatic Updates:**
- Quotes automatically fetch once every 24 hours
- Data is stored locally in your browser
- No personal data is collected or transmitted to external services (except the APIs)

**Manual Refresh:**
- Click the icon and press "Refresh Now" anytime to get fresh quotes

**Timestamps:**
- The popup shows when quotes were last updated

## Troubleshooting

**Extension not showing?**
- Make sure "Developer mode" is ON at chrome://extensions/
- Try unpacking and re-loading if issues persist

**No quotes showing?**
- Click "Refresh Now" to manually fetch
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page

**Need to unload?**
- Go to chrome://extensions/
- Click the "Remove" button on the extension card
